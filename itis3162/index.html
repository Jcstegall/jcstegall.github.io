About Me

Hi, I'm Julian Stegall, a senior at UNC Charlotte deeply interested in data science and its power to uncover patterns, solve problems, and improve the world. My academic path has taken me through computer science and statistics, and I've especially enjoyed courses in Python programming, statistics, and databases.

I'm eager to grow my skills in real-world data analysis, machine learning, and effective data storytelling. My goal in this class is to learn the practical tools and best practices of a modern data scientist—from data cleaning and visualization, to model building and communicating insights.

When not working with data, I love nature, climbing, hiking, biking, and sharing life with others. I'm excited to document my journey and projects here!
