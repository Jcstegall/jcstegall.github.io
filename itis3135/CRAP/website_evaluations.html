Website Evaluations

James Bond 007 Museum

This horrid website is a showcasing of the James Bond Museum website. The very long home page has a section with a border showcasing each individual item at the museum and other videos and information. If you are brave enough to click on one of the links, it will bring you to a page with more details about the item.

  • Contrast:The page has contrast due to its solely black and white nature, but it looks more like a government website from the 2000s than a website for a museum.
  • Repetition: The repetition is poor. The only consistency is the lack of styling, causing the same default fonts, borders, and black text.
  • Alignment: Zooming out on the webpage reveals design inconsistencies. Some boxes are centered while their content is not, while others are aligned randomly.
  • Proximity: Proximity is a strong suit as almost every piece of media has a description and a link to further explore.
